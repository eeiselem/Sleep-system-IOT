Environmental History — Sleep Sessions

SpO₂, HRV / PRV, ambient light (lux when provided), VOC / air-quality index, ambient noise (dB), heart rate, and movement share one synced time axis across pan and zoom.

Drag to pan horizontally; scroll or pinch on the charts to zoom the time axis.
